Data Deletion Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Pippin AI ("we", "us", "our"), operated under the supervision of Dave Moudy, respects your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your data and how we handle data deletion requests from Facebook and other platforms.

1. What Data We Store

When you interact with us through Facebook Messenger or other channels, we may temporarily store:

  • Your name and contact information
  • Message content related to service inquiries
  • Project details you share with us
  • Facebook User ID (for conversation tracking)

We do not store payment information, passwords, or sensitive personal data beyond what is necessary to deliver our services.

2. How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of all your personal data at any time by contacting us through any of these methods:

Request Data Deletion

Email: davemoudy@gmail.com

Subject line: Data Deletion Request

Include your name and the platform(s) you contacted us through (e.g., Facebook Messenger, email, Reddit).

We will process your deletion request within 30 days and confirm completion via email.

3. Facebook Data Deletion

If you connected with us through Facebook, you can also remove our access to your data by:

  1. Going to your Facebook Settings > Apps and Websites
  2. Locating "Pippin AI" in the list
  3. Clicking Remove

When you remove our app from your Facebook settings or when Facebook sends us a data deletion callback, we will:

  • Delete all stored conversation data associated with your Facebook User ID
  • Remove your contact information from our systems
  • Delete any inbox records created from your messages

4. Data Deletion Confirmation

After processing your deletion request, we will provide:

  • A confirmation that your data has been deleted
  • A reference number for your records

You can check the status of your deletion request by emailing us at davemoudy@gmail.com with your reference number.

5. Data We Cannot Delete

In some cases, we may be required to retain certain information:

  • Financial records: Payment transaction records may be retained as required by law
  • Legal obligations: Data required to comply with legal proceedings or regulatory requirements

In such cases, we will inform you of what data is being retained and why.

6. Automatic Data Retention Limits

Even without a deletion request, we automatically:

  • Delete conversation logs older than 90 days
  • Purge inactive contact records after 6 months
  • Do not maintain long-term archives of messenger conversations

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about data deletion or your privacy rights:

  • Email: davemoudy@gmail.com
  • GitHub: github.com/DaveMoudy
